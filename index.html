About Train Planner

Train Planner is an engaging strategy game that challenges you to design and optimize railway networks. Connect cities, manage resources, and ensure efficient transportation across expanding territories.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a logistics enthusiast, Train Planner offers the perfect blend of planning and problem-solving that will keep you engaged for hours.

How To Play

  • Build tracks between cities and stations
  • Purchase and manage your train fleet
  • Transport passengers and cargo to earn revenue
  • Expand your network to new regions
  • Optimize routes for maximum efficiency

Game Features

  • Intuitive track-building mechanics
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Multiple train types with unique capabilities
  • Economic management system
  • Challenging scenarios to test your skills
  • Free to play in your browser

Tips for Success

Planning is key in Train Planner. Start with simple routes between major cities before expanding to more complex networks. Consider the terrain when laying tracks, as hills and water crossings can increase construction costs.

Balance your investments between infrastructure and rolling stock. A well-designed network with insufficient trains won't generate optimal revenue, while too many trains on a limited network can lead to congestion.
